## Historical relevance

This preprint claimed undecidability of four-pair PCP and, as a corollary, mortality for five
`3 × 3` matrices and two `15 × 15` matrices. The accepted 2015 paper retreated to five PCP
pairs and six `3 × 3` matrices.

## Audit notes

Treat the headline result as abandoned, not as prior publication of a valid `M₃(5)` theorem.
ArXiv still serves v1 without a formal withdrawal notice; “superseded and erroneous” is the
accurate status, not “withdrawn.”
The local copy is retained because novelty claims must explicitly distinguish the new proposed
route from this earlier, invalid route to the same numerical frontier.
