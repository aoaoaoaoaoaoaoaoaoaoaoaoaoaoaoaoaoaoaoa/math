## Result Used

The paper proves decidability of the Skolem Problem for algebraic linear
recurrence sequences of order at most four, completing the remaining
order-four case.

This strengthens the available arithmetic machinery for low-dimensional
matrix-orbit and rank-drop analyses. In particular, scalar sequences of the
form `rᵀAⁿc` for `A` of dimension at most four are decidable for zero
occurrence.

## Scope and Hazards

The general Skolem Problem remains open, already at order five. The theorem
concerns a single exponent. It does not decide zero occurrence for arbitrary
products of several matrices or for the multi-exponent families arising after
unbounded alternation of rank-two matrices.
