## Results used

The introduction reports the accepted unrestricted-mortality bounds as six `3 × 3`, four
`5 × 5`, three `9 × 9`, and two `15 × 15` integer matrices, citing Cassaigne et al. This is
independent post-2015 evidence that the erroneous 2013 five-matrix announcement was not
regarded as settling `M₃(5)`.

The paper's own subject is mortality under the bounded-language constraint
`A₁^m₁ ⋯ Aₜ^mₜ`; none of its decidability results conflict with unrestricted `M₃(5)`.
