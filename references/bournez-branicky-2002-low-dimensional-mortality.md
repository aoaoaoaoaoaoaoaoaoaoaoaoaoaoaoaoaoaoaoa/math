## Results used

The paper proves decidability of mortality for two rational `2 × 2` matrices. Its minimal
zero-product lemma shows that a witness can be chosen with singular endpoint matrices and
only nonsingular matrices in its interior. It also relates unrestricted rational `2 × 2`
mortality to zero-in-the-corner and one-dimensional affine reachability problems.

## Audit notes

This is foundational for the dimension-two side of the campaign. The endpoint-rank lemma
explains why adding further singular generators is comparatively harmless: the unresolved core
is the semigroup generated by several nonsingular matrices acting between two rank-one
boundaries. The paper contains and discusses corrections to earlier low-dimensional arguments;
later work should be checked before reusing any isolated formula.
