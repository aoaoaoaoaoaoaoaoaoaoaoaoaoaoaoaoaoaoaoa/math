## Results used

The slides state and outline decidability of mortality for arbitrary finite `2 × 2`
integer families whose nonsingular members lie in `GL(2,ℤ)`, equivalently whose
determinants all belong to `{0, 1, −1}`. They reduce a minimal mortal word to two
singular endpoints around a product of unimodular matrices, then use the effective regular
subset theory of `GL(2,ℤ)`.

## Audit notes

The stored PDF is the authors' 24-frame conference slide deck, not the six-page proceedings
article. The peer-reviewed result is independently restated and reproved as Theorem 11 of
Potapov and Semukhin (2017), which is the source this project should cite for technical use.
The deck is retained as the lawfully public precursor and for its concise proof anatomy.
