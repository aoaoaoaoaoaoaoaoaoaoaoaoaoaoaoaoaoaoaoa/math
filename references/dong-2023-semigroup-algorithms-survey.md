## Results used

This survey places mortality among the principal algorithmic problems for matrix semigroups.
Its Theorem 3.6 records Paterson's undecidability result for a finite, unbounded number of
`3 × 3` integer generators. It does not state a sharper fixed-generator frontier and does
not contain the terminal-tile absorption argument.

## Audit notes

This is contextual rather than load-bearing evidence. Its silence is weak novelty evidence,
not proof that the present result was unknown.
